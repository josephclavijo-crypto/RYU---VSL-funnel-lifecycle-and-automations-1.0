Purpose

This automation runs right after a lead submits the VSL opt-in form. Its job is to filter out bad-fit contacts early (invalid emails, Microsoft inboxes that often block deliverability, leads who already booked a discovery call, and non-MQLs) and then nurture qualified leads with a 3-email sequence spaced one day apart, re-checking the same four exit conditions before every send so no one gets emailed after they've become disqualified, scheduled, or unreachable mid-sequence.

Simplified overview

Trigger: Form submitted │ ▼ Action: Wait 1 minute │ ▼ Action: Check email + stage + MQL status │ ▼ IF qualified (valid email, no Microsoft, not scheduled, is MQL) │ ▼ Action: Send email (1 of 3, repeats with 1-day waits) │ ▼ End: Sequence complete
